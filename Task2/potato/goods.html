Bellarosa

The variety of potato "Bellarosa" launched by German breeders and successfully cultivated in temperate climate conditions. Officially in the State Register of cultivated plants, recommended for cultivation in the territory of Eastern Europe, a variety introduced in the early 2000s.

Jersey Royal

The Jersey Royal is a type of potato grown in Jersey which has a Protected Designation of Origin. The potatoes are of the variety known as International Kidney and are typically grown as a new potato.
