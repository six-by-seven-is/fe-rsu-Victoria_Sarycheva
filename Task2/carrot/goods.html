Carrot cultivars

Red-Cored Chantenay

Chantenay carrots are short and stout, with broad 4-8cm crowns tapering quickly to a rounded point 15cm away.

‘Red-Cored Chantenay’ produces deep-orange carrots with the classic cone shape of Chantaney carrots. Grows well in heavy soils, stays sweet in storage.

Kaleidoscope Mix

This is a multicolor mix of red, purple, yellow, and orange 18-20cm carrots. Contains equal parts of 'Atomic Red', 'Bambino', 'Cosmic Purple', 'Lunar White', and 'Solar Yellow' seeds. A Burpee Seeds customer favorite.
